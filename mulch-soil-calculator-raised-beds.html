Understanding your result

Mulch, topsoil, compost, garden mix, and potting media have different uses and settle differently. Measure the empty depth actually being filled; do not automatically fill an entire tall bed with premium soil.

Frequently asked questions

How many bags fill a raised bed?

Divide adjusted cubic feet by the volume printed on the bag and round up. Weight is not a reliable volume conversion because moisture and density vary.

Should I add extra for settling?

Loose organic material commonly settles, but the amount depends on composition, moisture, compaction, and time. Use the adjustable factor as a purchasing allowance, not a guarantee.
