Understanding your result

Actual layout controls offcuts and seams. Fire-rated assemblies, wet locations, ceilings, garages, multiple layers, curved surfaces, and local code may require different board types or quantities.

Frequently asked questions

Should doors and windows be subtracted?

Yes for a net-area estimate, but small openings may not reduce sheet purchases because surrounding cuts still consume boards.

How much joint compound is needed?

Coverage varies with finish level, bead, texture, installer technique, and product. Enter the manufacturer’s coverage instead of relying on one universal assumption.
