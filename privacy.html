Privacy policy

Effective: 13 July 2026

Calculator inputs are processed in your browser and are not submitted to a CalcSmart application server. No account is required.

The site stores only your light/dark theme preference in local browser storage. It does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking scripts in this release.

If you use a social-sharing button, your browser opens the selected third-party service. That service applies its own privacy policy; CalcSmart does not transmit calculator inputs to it.

Infrastructure providers such as Cloudflare may process routine request data for security and delivery under their own terms.
