Understanding your results

Logo churn differs from revenue churn. MRR is recurring subscription revenue, not bookings, cash, ARR, or GAAP revenue. Keep time windows, customer definitions, gross-margin treatment, and acquisition cost allocation consistent before using metrics in a pitch deck.

Frequently asked questions

What happens when churn is zero?

The simple reciprocal model produces an unbounded LTV, which is not credible evidence of infinite value. Use a longer cohort window or a capped forecasting horizon.

What is a good LTV:CAC ratio?

No universal ratio is appropriate. Stage, payback, retention quality, capital cost, sales cycle, growth rate, and gross margin change the interpretation.
