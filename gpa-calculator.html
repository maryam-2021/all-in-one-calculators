GPA Calculator

Calculate a credit-weighted GPA on a 4.0 scale.

Formula and method

Calculate a credit-weighted GPA on a 4.0 scale. The result is calculated with the relationship below and rounded only for display.

GPA = Σ(grade points × credits) ÷ Σ credits

Worked example

Example calculation

Grades 4.0 and 3.0 in equal-credit courses produce a 3.5 GPA.
