Frequently asked questions

How many 80 lb bags make one cubic yard?

At QUIKRETE’s nominal 0.60 cu ft yield, 45 bags equal 27 cu ft. Confirm the exact product label and round up to full bags.

Does a driveway need rebar?

Reinforcement depends on design and local requirements. This page only totals the length of a spacing value the user supplies; it does not select bar size, spacing, cover, laps, or reinforcement type.
