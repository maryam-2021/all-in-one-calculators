About TrueCalco

TrueCalco is a collection of browser-based calculators and converters. Inputs are processed on your device; no account is required.

How results are produced

Each tool uses the formula shown on its page. Results are estimates and can be affected by rounding, assumptions, units, dates, and the values you enter. Entertainment tools are labelled as fun and are not scientific assessments.

Finance, tax, benefits, health, pregnancy, and nutrition tools are educational aids—not professional advice, diagnoses, official quotes, or filing tools. Check material decisions with an authoritative source or qualified professional.

Quality and corrections

The recovered calculator set was reviewed on 13 July 2026. A previously non-functional currency tool was replaced with a transparent user-supplied-rate calculation, unsupported usage claims were removed, and high-stakes approximations were labelled more clearly.
